MySims Skyheroes
MySims Skyheroes is the sixth and final installment in the MySims Series. It was developed by Behavior Interactive and published by Electronic Arts (EA) on September 28, 2010. In it, the setting is based in the skies, utilizing the aesthetic of aircrafts. The player must lead the resistance in order to stop Morcubus and his drone army from taking over the skyways. Customize your plane with wings and engines obtained from finishing missions, or play in different modes such as solo campaign and boss battles, and local co-op or online multiplayer!
